First Aid for Seizures

⚠️ If a seizure lasts more than 5 minutes or the person doesn’t wake up, call emergency services immediately.

During a Seizure

  • 1️⃣ Stay calm and time the seizure.
  • 2️⃣ Clear the area of any dangerous objects.
  • 3️⃣ Cushion their head with something soft.
  • 4️⃣ Loosen any tight clothing around their neck.
  • 5️⃣ Turn them onto their side if possible (recovery position).

After a Seizure

  • 1️⃣ Check their breathing and keep them on their side.
  • 2️⃣ Stay with them until they are fully conscious.
  • 3️⃣ Reassure them as they may be confused.
  • 4️⃣ Help them get home safely if needed.

DO NOT

  • ❌ Restrain the person.
  • ❌ Put anything in their mouth.
  • ❌ Try to move them unless they are in danger.
  • ❌ Give them food or water until fully alert.

When to Call Emergency Services

  • ⚠️ The seizure lasts longer than 5 minutes.
  • ⚠️ The person does not wake up after the seizure.
  • ⚠️ The person has another seizure shortly after the first.
  • ⚠️ The person is injured during the seizure.
  • ⚠️ The person is pregnant or has diabetes.
  • ⚠️ This is the person’s first seizure.
