Seizure Detection

Generalized Tonic-Clonic Seizure

Characterized by loss of consciousness, muscle stiffening, and rhythmic jerking movements. Lasts 1-3 minutes.

Absence Seizure

Brief episodes of staring and unresponsiveness. May last 5-30 seconds.

Focal Seizure

Affects one part of the brain, causing unusual sensations or behaviors. Consciousness may be altered.

Emergency Response

Do not restrain the person. Protect their head and let them rest on their side after the seizure.
